About Us

In light of recent global events, the future of on-site retail shopping is more uncertain than it has ever been. Our team has identified the need for many businesses to transition to an online platform in order to continue reaching customers, and we've created an application to do just that. At Skip-the-Door, we're giving the showcase back to businesses. With our quick and easy uploading feature, owners can have their company's information, products, and images on display in a matter of minutes. Our unique search function allows customers to find any and all products a business has listed, and our user-friendly interface caters to everyone.
